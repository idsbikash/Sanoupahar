About The Movement

I am Bikash Timilsena, a concerned citizen dedicated to fighting corruption and injustice in Nepal. This platform serves to expose corruption at all levels and advocate for systemic change.

Through investigative journalism and citizen engagement, we're building a movement that will eventually lead to a political transformation in our country. Our goal is to create a Nepal where leaders are accountable, institutions are transparent, and citizens are empowered.

While I'm not currently a candidate for any office, this movement lays the groundwork for future political change. Join us in this journey toward a more just and equitable Nepal.
